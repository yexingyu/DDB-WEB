Administrative Agent

The Lender and the Borrower appoint Dailydealsbox Inc. ("DDB") as their sole administrative agent (the "Administrative Agent") for loan origination, bookkeeping and mediation. All communication between the Lender and the Borrower shall be conducted through the Administrative Agent. The Administrative Agent charges to the Borrower a one-time $19 fee for loan origination, which can be waived if certain conditions are met. The basic fee is 1% of the total Loan Amount to the Lender, which shall be paid with the loan proceeds disbursement. Please refer to fee schedules on DDB website for charges for additional services.

Loan Amount & Interest

1.       The Lender promises to loan $600.00 CAD to the Borrower and the Borrower promises to repay this principal amount to the Lender, with interest payable on the unpaid principal at the rate of 2.00 percent per month, calculated monthly not in advance.

2.       The loan proceeds will be disbursed to DDB's trust account first and will be applied to the purchase of the Merchandise from the third-party Merchant. The borrower will not receive any funds. The Merchandise will be shipped directly to the Borrower's shipping address on the Borrower's DDB profile. The Borrower confirms the accuracy of the address and will be responsible for any losses or damages from the point when the Merchandise is handed to the commercial carrier. DDB does not take title to the Merchandise.

Payment

3.       This Loan will be repaid in consecutive monthly instalments of principal and interest on the twenty-fourth day of each month commencing the month following execution of this Agreement and continuing until May 24th, 2016 with the balance then owing under this Agreement being paid at that time. The Borrower will provide to the Administrative Agent twelve signed checks (payable to Joe Lender), dated on May 24th of each of the twelve months following the loan date.

4.       At any time while not in default under this Agreement, the Borrower may pay the outstanding balance then owing under this Agreement to the Lender without further bonus or penalty.

 

Default

5.       Notwithstanding anything to the contrary in this Agreement, if the Borrower defaults in the performance of any obligation under this Agreement, then the Lender may declare the principal amount owing and interest due under this Agreement at that time to be immediately due and payable.

6.       Further, if the Lender declares the principle amount owing under this Agreement to be immediately due and payable, and the Borrower fails to provide full payment, interest in the amount of 2.5 percent per month, calculated monthly not in advance, will be charged on the outstanding amount, commencing the day the principal amount is declared due and payable, until full payment is received by the lender.

7.       If the Borrower defaults in payment as required under this Agreement or after demand for ten (10) days, the Security will be immediately provided to the Lender and the Lender is granted all rights of repossession as a secured party. The Borrower assumes all costs related to the repossession.

Security

8.       This Loan is secured by following security (the "Security"): the Merchandise.

9.       The Borrower grants to the Lender a security interest in the Security until this Loan is paid in full. The Lender will be listed as a lender on the title of the Security whether or not the Lender elects to perfect the security interest in the Security. The Borrower will do everything necessary to assist the Lender in perfecting its security interest.

Address Tips

Are there any tips to ensure fast delivery of my order to the correct address?

Enter your address information carefully. Ensure it is correct, accurate, and up-to-date. Incorrect address information will cause delays with your order.

  • Shipping address can be any address in Canada and should include the apartment number (if applicable)
  • Billing address and phone number must be identical to what's on your monthly credit card statement
  • Include the apartment buzzer number at the end of the address (2410 Mountain Rd. - Buzzer #120) (if applicable)
  • To make sure that orders being sent to PO boxes and rural route addresses get to their proper destination, please include a physical street address or highway number with your order
  • Scheduled Delivery items require a civic address. Delays will occur if only a PO box is provided as the shipping location

    Here are some common examples:

    Standard address: 2410 Mountain Rd.
    Standard address with apartment and buzzer code: 2410 Mountain Rd. Apt. 12 - Buzzer #120
    Standard PO BOX Address: PO BOX 4001 STN A 2410 Mountain Rd.

    To make sure that orders being sent to PO boxes and rural route addresses get to their proper destination, please include a physical street address or highway number in addition to your PO BOX number as shown above.

    • Please note: Scheduled Delivery items require a civic address. Delays will occur if only a PO box is provided as the shipping location.
